# Project Roles & Responsibilities

The following is an overview of the roles that contributors play within the Picard Documentation project along with a brief description of the responsibilities and expectations of each of the roles.

## Editor

The editor is responsible for the overall content, structure, organization and presentation of the documentation. The editor is also responsible for preparing updated translation template `*.pot` files for use by the translators, and for publishing the documentation. The editor provides reviews of proposed changes, and approval with respect to those identified responsibilities.

## Maintainers

Maintainers are responsible for assisting the Editor as required, including performing any of the duties normally provided by the Editor in the Editor's absence.

## Reviewers

Reviewers are responsible for helping to ensure the quality of the documentation, both from a technical perspective and grammatical perspective. Reviewers provide reviews of proposed changes, and approval with respect to their areas of expertise.

## Translators

Translators are responsible for translating the documentation from the base language of English to another supported language.

## Content Contributor

Content contributors are responsible for providing changes (additions, corrections and deletions) to the documentation. Contributions are submitted via pull requests, and are applied to the documentation’s base language of English. Content contributors are also responsible for working with reviewers to ensure a quality product by responding to comments and requests related to their pull requests.
